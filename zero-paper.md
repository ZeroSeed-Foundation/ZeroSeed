# Zero Paper

**Zero Seed is a civilizational-scale decentralized coordination layer to mitigate existential risks—locally, regionally, and globally.**

---

## What it is

Zero Seed creates an alternative system not in order to replace the existing one but to make the whole more robust. The Zero Seed layer is acyclical: it sits beneath the churn of politics, ideology, and nation, and supports what is durable rather than competing with it.

It is not a tribe, not a community, not a place of belonging—but it exists to make the flourishing of tribes, community, and belonging possible.

## Grounding

Zero Seed is **rooted in grounded action**: imagination and deep reflection in service of what can be built and sustained.

Zero Seed is the layer that seeks to create a common ground and, in doing so, seeks to avoid paradigmatic locking: it does not fix itself to any single frame of nation, ideology, or politics, so it can remain responsive to what is durable beneath them.

## Why it is not mainstream

Zero Seed is not mainstream, and it does not need to be. By definition it is set up to mitigate against the collapse of the mainstream. It is optimized for resilience and may not always seek optimal efficiency.
